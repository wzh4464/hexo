# CUR Decomposition

$$
A \simeq C U R \\
C = A(:,q) \\
R = A(p,:) \\
U = C^\dagger A R^\dagger \\
p \text{ is a subset of } \{1, \dots, m\} \\
q \text{ is a subset of } \{1, \dots, n\} \\
$$

$$
A  \in \mathbb{R}^{m \times n} \\
A = VSW^\top \\
S \in \mathbb{R}^{k \times k} \\
$$

DEIM then is used to find $p$ and $q$ from $V$ and $W$.
